Chef's Manual

Perfecting Plant Protein Searing

Because plant proteins contain lower water density and clean vegetable oils, they cook faster than standard animal meat cuts.

Preheat Griddle First

Ensure cast-iron skillets or grills are hot and lightly oiled before laying patties. This locks in internal juices.

Avoid Pressing the Patty

Do not press the burger patty with a spatula while grilling. This squeezes out the marbling oils, making the burger dry.
